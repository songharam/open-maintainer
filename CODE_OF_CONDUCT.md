# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Open Maintainer Workbench to be useful, focused, and welcoming for people who maintain open source projects.

Contributors and maintainers are expected to communicate respectfully, assume good intent, and keep discussion centered on the project goals.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear, constructive, and specific.
- Respect maintainers' time.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Help make the project easier for new contributors to understand.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Dismissive or hostile feedback.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic discussion after maintainers ask to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that do not follow this code of conduct. Repeated violations may lead to temporary or permanent removal from project participation.
